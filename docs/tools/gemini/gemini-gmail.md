# Gemini in Gmail

Gemini in Gmail helps you manage email faster by summarizing long threads and drafting replies.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Summarizing email threads

When you open a long email thread with many replies:

1. Look for the **"Summarize this email"** button at the top of the thread (sparkle icon).
2. Click it — Gemini reads all the messages and gives you a concise summary with key takeaways and any action items.

This is a lifesaver for threads with 10+ messages where you just need to know "what was decided?"

---

## Drafting replies with "Help me write"

1. Click **"Reply"** on an email.
2. Click the **Gemini sparkle icon** in the compose toolbar (or look for "Help me write").
3. Type a brief prompt: "Accept the meeting but ask to move it to Thursday" or "Politely decline and suggest next week."
4. Gemini drafts a reply. Review, edit, and send.

You can also refine the draft: "Make it more formal" or "Shorten it."

---

## Smart reply suggestions

Gemini enhances Gmail's smart reply suggestions with more contextual, relevant options based on the conversation. Look for suggested replies at the bottom of emails.

---

## Practical use cases

| Task | Prompt Example |
|------|----------------|
| Responding to parent emails | "Write a professional reply acknowledging the concern and offering to schedule a conference" |
| Replying to admin requests | "Accept and confirm I'll have the data ready by Friday" |
| Following up | "Write a friendly follow-up asking if they had a chance to review my request" |
