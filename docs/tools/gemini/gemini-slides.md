# Gemini in Slides

Gemini in Google Slides can help you create slides, generate original images, and design presentations faster.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Creating slides with Gemini

1. Open a **Google Slides** presentation.
2. Click the **Gemini sparkle icon** in the toolbar or open the side panel.
3. Describe what you want: "Create a title slide for a presentation about the solar system" or "Add a slide summarizing the causes of World War I."
4. Gemini generates slide content that you can insert and customize.

---

## Generating images

Gemini can create original images directly in your presentation:

1. In the Gemini side panel, describe the image you want: "A colorful illustration of the water cycle for a science class."
2. Gemini generates several image options.
3. Click to insert your favorite into the slide.

!!! tip "Great for when you can't find the right clip art"
    Instead of searching for stock images, describe exactly what you need. The generated images are original and can be tailored to your lesson.

---

## Practical use cases

| Task | What to Ask Gemini |
|------|--------------------|
| Building a presentation | "Create an 8-slide presentation outline about the American Civil War for 8th graders" |
| Adding visuals | "Generate an image showing the three branches of government" |
| Creating a summary slide | "Create a summary slide with the 5 key takeaways from this presentation" |
