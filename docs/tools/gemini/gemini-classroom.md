# Gemini in Google Classroom

Gemini is now integrated directly into Google Classroom, providing AI-powered tools to help educators create resources, plan lessons, and differentiate content — all from one central location.

**Time to complete:** ~8 min

---

## Accessing Gemini in Classroom

1. Open **Google Classroom**.
2. Look for the **Gemini tab** in the navigation bar.
3. Click it to access AI-powered resource creation tools.

!!! info "Availability"
    Gemini in Classroom is available to educators 18+ across all Google Workspace for Education editions. Your admin must have Gemini in Classroom enabled.

---

## What Gemini can do in Classroom

Gemini in Classroom includes 30+ AI-powered tools for common educator tasks. Some highlights:

### Create first drafts of resources

- **Lesson plans** based on learning objectives
- **Vocabulary lists** with definitions and example sentences
- **Reading passages** adjusted to specific grade levels
- **Discussion prompts** for class conversations
- **Rubrics** for grading assignments
- **Study guides** summarizing key concepts

### Differentiate content

- Take a complex text and **adjust it to a different reading level**
- Create **multiple versions** of the same content for different learners
- Generate materials that **address common misconceptions**

### Refine and iterate

After Gemini generates content, you can ask it to refine the output in a chat-like experience: "Make it shorter," "Add more examples," "Adjust for ELL students."

---

## Assigning generated content

Once you've reviewed and edited Gemini's output:

1. Click **"Add to class"**.
2. Select the **class** where you want to assign it.
3. Classroom creates an assignment with the generated content attached as a Google Drive file.

!!! warning "Always review before assigning"
    Gemini creates drafts. Always read through the content, check for accuracy, adjust for your specific students, and make it your own before sending to students.

---

## Starter prompts

Gemini in Classroom provides pre-built prompt cards for common tasks like:

- Gamifying an activity
- Creating DOK (Depth of Knowledge) questions
- Writing bell ringers or exit tickets
- Generating differentiated reading materials

Click a card and Gemini loads a pre-set prompt that you can customize and iterate on.
