# Gemini for Workspace

Gemini is Google's AI assistant built directly into the Google Workspace tools you already use — Docs, Gmail, Slides, Sheets, Forms, and Google Classroom. It can help you draft content, summarize documents, generate ideas, create images, and save time on repetitive tasks.

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - Gemini features are available to **staff aged 18+** with SCPS Google Workspace accounts. Your admin controls which Gemini features are enabled.
    - **Your data is protected**: When using Gemini with your SCPS school account, your data is not used to train AI models and is covered under Google Workspace for Education terms.
    - **Always review AI-generated content** before sharing with students. Gemini is a drafting tool, not a finished-product generator.
    - Check with your ITRT about which Gemini features are currently enabled in your SCPS account — availability depends on your Workspace edition and admin settings.
