# What Is Gemini and How to Access It

Gemini comes in several forms across Google's ecosystem. This guide clarifies what's what and how to access each one.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## The three faces of Gemini

| Form | What It Is | How to Access |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **Gemini app** | A standalone AI chatbot (like ChatGPT) for brainstorming, research, and generating content | Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) or use the Gemini mobile app |
| **Gemini in Workspace** | AI features built into Docs, Gmail, Slides, Sheets, and Forms | Look for "Help me write," "Help me create," or the Gemini sparkle icon inside each app |
| **Gemini in Classroom** | AI-powered lesson planning tools inside Google Classroom | Open Google Classroom and look for the Gemini tab |

---

## Accessing Gemini with your SCPS account

1. Make sure you're **signed into your SCPS Google account** (not a personal account).
2. For the Gemini app: go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com).
3. For Gemini in Workspace apps: open Docs, Gmail, Slides, etc. — look for the Gemini sparkle icon or "Help me write" prompts.

!!! warning "Feature availability"
    Not all Gemini features may be enabled for your SCPS account. If you don't see Gemini options in your apps, check with your ITRT — your admin may need to enable them.

---

## Data privacy

When using Gemini with your school account:

- Your data is **not used to train AI models**
- Your data is **not reviewed by humans** to improve Gemini
- Gemini has the same **enterprise-grade data protection** as Gmail and Docs
- It's covered under **Google Workspace for Education terms**, including FERPA and COPPA compliance
