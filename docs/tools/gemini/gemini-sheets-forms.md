# Gemini in Sheets and Forms

Gemini can help you create spreadsheet templates, organize data in Google Sheets, and build assessments in Google Forms.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Gemini in Google Sheets

### Creating tables and templates

1. Open a **Google Sheet**.
2. Click the **Gemini icon** or open the side panel.
3. Describe what you need: "Create a grade tracker template with columns for student name, assignment 1 through 5, average, and letter grade."
4. Gemini generates the structure. Insert it and customize.

### Organizing data

Gemini can help with data tasks:

- "Create a formula to calculate the average of cells B2 through B30"
- "Sort this data by the scores column from highest to lowest"
- "Highlight cells in column C that are below 70"

!!! tip "Ask Gemini for formulas"
    If you're not a spreadsheet expert, Gemini can write formulas for you. Just describe what you want in plain English.

---

## Gemini in Google Forms

Gemini can help create assessments and surveys faster:

1. Open **Google Forms**.
2. Look for the **Gemini feature** (if available in your account).
3. Describe what you need: "Create a 10-question quiz about the American Revolution for 8th graders with multiple choice and short answer questions."
4. Gemini generates questions that you can review and edit.

Gemini can also generate a Form based on an existing Google Doc, Slide deck, or PDF from your Drive, and summarize form responses after students submit.
