# Exporting and Sharing with Students

Once you've generated and customized your Diffit materials, you need to get them into students' hands. Diffit integrates with the tools your students already use.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Export options

Diffit supports direct export to:

| Format | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Google Docs** | Editable documents students can annotate or type responses into |
| **Google Slides** | Presentation-style delivery of content |
| **Google Forms** | Auto-graded quizzes from the comprehension questions |
| **Google Classroom** | Post directly as an assignment in your Google Classroom |
| **Microsoft 365** | Word, PowerPoint, and other Microsoft formats |
| **PDF** | Printable worksheets for paper-based activities |

---

## Exporting to Google Classroom

The fastest workflow for SCPS teachers:

1. After generating your materials, click **"Export"** or **"Send to"**.
2. Select **"Google Classroom"**.
3. Choose the **class** and set it up as an assignment.
4. The materials are automatically attached.
5. Students see it in their Classwork page.

---

## Exporting to Google Forms (quizzes)

If you want the comprehension questions auto-graded:

1. Select the questions you want to include.
2. Export as **"Google Forms"**.
3. The form is created with questions and answer keys pre-populated.
4. Assign through Google Classroom or share the form link.

---

## All exports stay editable

No matter which format you choose, the exported files remain **fully editable**. You can:

- Add your own content alongside Diffit's generated material
- Adjust formatting, add images, or rearrange sections
- Mix Diffit materials from different passages into one document

!!! tip "Build a resource library"
    Save your exported Diffit materials in a Google Drive folder organized by unit. Over time, you'll build a library of differentiated resources you can reuse and share with colleagues.
