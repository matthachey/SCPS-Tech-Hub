# Getting Started with Diffit

Diffit turns any topic, article, or video into differentiated instructional materials in minutes. This guide walks you through setting up your account and understanding the basics.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Creating your account

1. Go to **[app.diffit.me](https://app.diffit.me)**.
2. Click **"Sign up"** and create an account using your **SCPS Google account** (or email).
3. Complete the brief setup — select your role (teacher), grade level, and subject area.

---

## The Diffit workflow

The basic flow is simple:

1. **Input** — Give Diffit something to work with (a topic, article, video, or text)
2. **Configure** — Choose the grade level, reading level, language, and standards
3. **Generate** — Diffit creates differentiated materials
4. **Review & edit** — Check the content and make adjustments
5. **Export** — Send to Google Docs, Classroom, Forms, or download as PDF

---

## Ways to input content

| Input Method | How It Works |
|--------------|--------------|
| **Topic** | Type any topic (e.g., "photosynthesis" or "causes of the Civil War") and Diffit generates reading material from scratch |
| **Article/URL** | Paste a web article URL and Diffit adapts it to your chosen reading level |
| **YouTube video** | Paste a YouTube link and Diffit creates a reading passage based on the video content |
| **Paste text** | Copy and paste any text you want adapted |
| **Upload a file** | Upload a document or PDF to be adapted |

---

## Choosing your settings

Before generating, you'll configure:

| Setting | What to Choose |
|---------|----------------|
| **Grade level** | 2nd grade through 12th grade and above |
| **Language** | English, Spanish, French, and more — great for ELL support |
| **Standards** | Optionally align to specific standards (Virginia SOLs, Common Core, etc.) |
| **Reading level** | Diffit adjusts text complexity to match your selection |
