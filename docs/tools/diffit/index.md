# Diffit

Diffit is an AI-powered tool that helps teachers create differentiated reading materials, comprehension questions, vocabulary lists, and activities from any text, topic, video, or website. It takes the heavy lifting out of differentiation so every student can access grade-level content at an appropriate reading level.

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - Diffit offers a **free tier** with several resources per month. Check with your ITRT about whether SCPS has a premium license.
    - Diffit exports directly to **Google Classroom, Google Docs, Google Slides, Google Forms, and PDF** — all tools your students already use.
    - **Always review AI-generated content** for accuracy before sharing with students. Diffit is a time-saving tool, not a replacement for your expertise.
    - Diffit is especially valuable for **ELL students and students with IEPs** who need access to grade-level content at modified reading levels.
