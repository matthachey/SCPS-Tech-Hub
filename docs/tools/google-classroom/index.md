# Google Classroom

Google Classroom is the assignment and communication hub built into Google Workspace for Education. It integrates tightly with Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, and Drive, making it easy to create, distribute, collect, and grade student work — all within the Google ecosystem your students already know.

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - Google Classroom classes may be **auto-provisioned from your SIS** or you may need to create them manually — check with your building ITRT.
    - Google Classroom rosters feed into **Lightspeed Classroom** for device monitoring. Make sure students have joined your Google Classroom before using Lightspeed.
    - SCPS uses **Canvas as the primary LMS**. Google Classroom is commonly used alongside Canvas for lightweight assignments, Google Docs workflows, and communication. Clarify with your building which platform is the primary gradebook of record.
    - **Guardian summaries**: Parents/guardians can receive email summaries of their student's work — encourage families to opt in.
