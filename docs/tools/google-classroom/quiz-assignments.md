# Creating Quiz Assignments with Google Forms

Google Forms integrates directly with Classroom to create auto-graded quizzes. Scores sync automatically to your Grades page.

**Time to complete:** ~8 min

---

## Creating a quiz assignment

1. Go to **Classwork** → **"+ Create"** → **"Quiz assignment"**.
2. A Google Form is automatically created and attached.
3. Fill in the assignment title, instructions, points, due date, and topic.
4. Click the **Google Form attachment** (called "Blank Quiz") to open the Forms editor.

---

## Building your quiz in Google Forms

### Adding questions

1. Click **"+"** to add a new question.
2. Choose a question type:

| Type | Auto-Graded? | Best For |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Multiple choice** | Yes | Standard quiz questions |
| **Checkbox** | Yes | "Select all that apply" |
| **Short answer** | Yes (with answer key) | Fill-in-the-blank, single-word answers |
| **Paragraph** | No — needs manual grading | Open-ended responses |
| **Dropdown** | Yes | Select from a list |

### Setting up the answer key

1. At the bottom of each question, click **"Answer key"**.
2. Select the **correct answer(s)**.
3. Set the **point value** for the question.
4. Optionally add **answer feedback** — different messages for correct and incorrect responses.

!!! tip "Answer feedback is powerful"
    Write a brief explanation for incorrect answers: "Not quite — remember that the Constitution was ratified in 1788, not 1776." Students see this immediately after submitting, turning every wrong answer into a learning moment.

---

## Quiz settings in Google Forms

Click the **gear icon** (Settings) in Google Forms:

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Release grade** | "Immediately after each submission" for formative; "Later, after manual review" for summative |
| **Respondent can see** | Choose what students see after submitting: missed questions, correct answers, point values |
| **Limit to 1 response** | Turn on for formal assessments |
| **Shuffle question order** | Turn on to reduce copying |

---

## How grades sync to Classroom

When students complete the quiz:

1. Auto-graded questions are scored immediately.
2. Grades appear on the **Grades page** in Classroom.
3. You can also **import grades** from Forms to Classroom: open the assignment → click **"Import Grades"**.

For paragraph/open-ended questions, you'll need to grade those manually in Google Forms and then import the updated grades.
