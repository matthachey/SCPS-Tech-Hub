# Setting Up Your Class

Before posting assignments, take a few minutes to configure your class settings. This ensures grades calculate correctly and students have a smooth experience.

**Time to complete:** ~8 min

---

## Accessing class settings

1. Open your class.
2. Click the **gear icon** (Settings) in the top right corner.

---

## Configuring your grading system

Under **"Grading"** in Settings, choose how overall grades are calculated:

| Option | How It Works | Best For |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| **Total Points** | Total points earned ÷ total points possible = overall grade | Simple grading — all assignments weighted equally by their point value |
| **Weighted by Category** | You assign percentage weights to grade categories | Weighting categories differently (e.g., Tests worth more than Homework) |
| **No Overall Grade** | No grade is calculated for students | When you don't want students seeing an overall percentage |

### Setting up weighted categories

If you choose **Weighted by Category**:

1. Click **"Add Grade Category"**.
2. Name the category (e.g., `Classwork`, `Assessments`, `Homework`).
3. Assign a **percentage** weight.
4. Repeat for all categories. **Weights must total 100%.**
5. Click **"Save"**.

!!! warning "Match your school's grading policy"
    Make sure your categories and weights match what your building requires. Check with your administration before setting up your own system.

## Other important settings

| Setting | What to Do |
|---------|------------|
| **Class code** | Students use this to join your class. You can reset or disable it after everyone has joined. |
| **Stream** | Choose who can post and comment on the Stream. "Only teacher can post or comment" keeps it clean. |
| **Classwork on Stream** | Choose whether classwork posts appear on the Stream. Turning this on keeps students informed; turning it off keeps the Stream clean. |
| **Show deleted items** | Leave this off unless you need to recover something. |
| **Guardian summaries** | Enable guardian email summaries so parents receive updates about their student's work. |

---

## Inviting students

There are two ways to add students:

=== "Class code"
    1. Share the **class code** (found in Settings or on the Stream banner).
    2. Students go to classroom.google.com → click **"+"** → **"Join class"** → enter the code.

=== "Email invite"
    1. Go to the **People** tab.
    2. Click the **invite icon** next to "Students."
    3. Enter student email addresses.

---

## Inviting guardians

Guardians receive email summaries of their student's upcoming and missing work.

1. Go to the **People** tab.
2. Next to a student's name, click the **three dots** → **"Invite guardian"**.
3. Enter the guardian's email address.

!!! tip "Encourage guardian opt-in"
    Send a note home at the start of the year asking families for their email addresses for Google Classroom guardian summaries. This dramatically reduces "I didn't know about the assignment" conversations.
