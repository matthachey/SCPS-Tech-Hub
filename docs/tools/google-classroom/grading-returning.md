# Grading and Returning Work

When students turn in work, it shows up for you to grade. Grades are saved as drafts until you return the assignment, at which point students can see their score.

**Time to complete:** ~8 min

---

## Viewing student submissions

1. Go to the **Classwork** tab.
2. Click on the assignment.
3. Click the number above **"Turned in"** to open the Student Work page.

You'll see all students organized by status:

| Status | Meaning | Color |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Turned in** | Student submitted their work | — |
| **Assigned** | Student hasn't submitted yet | — |
| **Graded** | You've entered a grade | — |
| **Missing** | Past due with no submission | Red |

---

## Grading from the Student Work page

1. Click on a **student's name** to open their submission.
2. Their attached file opens in the right panel — you can review it directly.
3. Enter a **grade** in the grade field on the right.
4. Optionally add a **private comment** for the student.
5. The grade saves as a **draft** until you return it.

### Adding comments and feedback

- **Private comments**: Only the student sees these. Use for individual feedback.
- **Annotation (on Google Docs)**: Open the student's Google Doc and add comments or suggestions directly on the document. Students see your comments when the assignment is returned.

!!! tip "Comment directly on the Doc"
    For Google Docs submissions, open the doc and use the **commenting and suggesting** tools built into Google Docs. This is more specific and actionable than a general comment in the grade field.

---

## Returning work

Grades are invisible to students until you **return** the assignment.

### Return to one student
1. On the Student Work page, check the box next to the student's name.
2. Click **"Return"**.
3. Confirm.

### Return to multiple students
1. Check the boxes next to multiple students (or select all).
2. Click **"Return"**.
3. All selected students receive their grade and any feedback.

!!! info "Students are notified"
    When you return work, students receive a notification (if they have notifications enabled). They can see their grade and your comments.

## Bulk grading with "Grade All"

For assignments graded on completion (exit tickets, classwork):

1. Go to the **Grades** tab.
2. Click the **three-dot menu** on the assignment column.
3. Click **"Grade all"**.
4. Enter the grade to apply to all students (e.g., `100`).
5. Optionally check **"Override existing grades"** and **"Automatically return after grading"**.
6. Click **"Apply"**.

!!! tip "Huge time saver"
    For completion-based assignments, "Grade All" lets you give every student the same score in one click. Combined with "Automatically return," the entire grading and returning process takes 5 seconds.

---

## Marking work as missing, excused, or complete

- **Missing**: Automatically applied when work is past due with no submission. You can also manually mark it.
- **Excused**: Removes the assignment from the student's grade calculation. Use for absent students or accommodations.
- **Complete**: Marks the assignment as done without a numeric grade.

To apply these: click the grade cell on the Grades page → use the dropdown options.
