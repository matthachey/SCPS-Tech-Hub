# PowerTeacher Pro

PowerTeacher Pro is the gradebook built into PowerSchool SIS. It's where your **official grades live** — the grades that go on report cards, transcripts, and the parent/student portal. Whether you grade directly in PowerTeacher Pro or sync grades from Canvas or Google Classroom, understanding this tool is essential.

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - PowerTeacher Pro is your **official gradebook of record**. Grades entered here appear on report cards, transcripts, and the PowerSchool parent/student portal.
    - **Use Chrome** for the best PowerTeacher Pro experience. It's fastest and most reliable in Chrome.
    - Grade categories may be **pre-set by the district**. Do not create your own categories if district categories have been provided — grades using custom categories may not calculate into the final grade correctly.
    - If you grade in Canvas or Google Classroom, you **must sync** those grades to PowerTeacher Pro for them to count on report cards.
