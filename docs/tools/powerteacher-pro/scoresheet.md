# Using the Scoresheet

The Scoresheet is the traditional gradebook grid — students as rows, assignments as columns. It's the fastest way to enter multiple grades at once.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Accessing the Scoresheet

1. Click **Grading** → **Scoresheet**.
2. The grid view opens with all students and assignments.

---

## Entering grades in the Scoresheet

1. **Click on any cell** to select it.
2. Type the score using your keyboard or the Score Inspector keypad.
3. Press **Tab** to move to the next student (down) or **Enter** to save.
4. Click **Save** when finished.

### Color coding

- Cells are **color-coded by category** — this helps you quickly identify which assignments belong to which category.
- You can customize category colors in the Categories settings.

---

## Viewing totals and averages

The Scoresheet shows **category totals** and **overall grade** columns:

- **Category Total** columns show the calculated grade for each category
- **Overall Grade** appears at the far right based on your weighting setup
- These update in real time as you enter scores

---

## Sorting and filtering

- Click a **column header** to sort by that assignment
- Use the **filter options** (gear icon) to show/hide specific categories or date ranges
- Toggle **"Show Category Totals"** on or off for a cleaner view

---

## Exporting the Scoresheet

1. Click the **export icon** (top right of the Scoresheet).
2. Choose **Excel** or **CSV** format.
3. Download your gradebook for backup or analysis.
