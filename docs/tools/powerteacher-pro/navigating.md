# Navigating PowerTeacher Pro

PowerTeacher Pro is accessed through the PowerSchool portal. This guide walks you through getting in and understanding the main interface.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## Accessing PowerTeacher Pro

1. Log in to the **PowerSchool portal** with your SCPS credentials.
2. From the navigation menu, click **"PowerTeacher Pro"** — or click it from beneath your course listing.
3. The gradebook opens showing your classes.

!!! tip "Use Chrome"
    PowerTeacher Pro runs fastest and most reliably in Google Chrome. If you're experiencing slowness or display issues, switch to Chrome.

---

## The main interface

### Class selector

When you first open PowerTeacher Pro, you'll see your **class list**. Click on any class to open its gradebook. You can customize class names and colors for easy identification.

### Key menu areas

| Area | What It Does |
|------|--------------|
| **Grading** | Access the Scoresheet, Assignments, Categories, and Standards pages |
| **Students** | View student demographics, schedules, and individual grade details |
| **Settings** | Configure Traditional Grade Calculations, Display Settings, and preferences |
| **Create** | Quick-create new assignments and categories |

### The Quick Menu

Look for **arrows next to page headings** — clicking these opens a Quick Menu with shortcuts to related areas. This saves a lot of clicking through menus.

---

## Switching between classes

Use the **class dropdown** at the top of the page to switch between your classes without going back to the main selector.

---

## Key icons and indicators

| Icon | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Lock icon** on an assignment column | The reporting term or class is locked — you cannot edit grades |
| **Blue comment icon** in a score cell | A comment exists for that student's grade |
| **Flag icons** (Late, Missing, Absent, etc.) | Score indicators that mark submission status |
| **Green checkmark** | Collected/completed |
