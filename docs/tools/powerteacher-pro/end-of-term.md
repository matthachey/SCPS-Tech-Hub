# Verifying Grades and End-of-Term Tasks

Before report cards go out, take time to verify your grades, add comments, and make sure everything in PowerTeacher Pro is accurate.

**Time to complete:** ~5 min

---

## End-of-term checklist

### 1. Verify all assignments are graded

1. Open the **Scoresheet**.
2. Look for any **empty cells** — these are missing grades.
3. Decide whether each should be a zero, exempt, absent, or missing flag.
4. Enter appropriate scores or flags for all remaining blanks.

!!! warning "Blanks are not zeros"
    In PowerTeacher Pro, a blank cell is **excluded from the grade calculation** — it doesn't count as a zero. If a student didn't submit an assignment and should receive a zero, you must enter `0` explicitly.

### 2. Check category totals and overall grades

1. On the Scoresheet, review the **category total** and **overall grade** columns.
2. Spot-check a few students to make sure the calculated grade matches what you expect.
3. If something looks off, check your **category weights** in Settings → Traditional Grade Calculations.

### 3. Add report card comments

1. Go to **Grading** → **Reporting Terms**.
2. Select the current term.
3. For each student, click in the **comment field**.
4. Type your comment or use the **Comment Bank** to select pre-written comments.
5. Click **Save**.

!!! info "Comment Bank"
    PowerTeacher Pro has a Comment Bank where you can save frequently used comments. Access it from the comment field. You can also import comments from previous years if you used the old PowerTeacher Gradebook.

### 4. Verify GradeSync (if using Canvas or Google Classroom)

If you sync grades from another platform:

1. Run a final **manual sync** from Canvas or export from Google Classroom.
2. Open PowerTeacher Pro and verify that the latest grades are reflected.
3. Check for any sync errors on the Grade Sync status page in Canvas.

### 5. Confirm grades are final

Once you've verified everything, your grades are ready for report cards. Your building admin will communicate the deadline for finalizing grades and when the reporting term will be locked.

!!! warning "Once locked, you can't edit"
    After the reporting term is locked by administration, you cannot change grades. Make all corrections before the deadline.
