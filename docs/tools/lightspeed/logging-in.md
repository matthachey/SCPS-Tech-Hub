# Logging In and Navigating the Dashboard

The Lightspeed Classroom dashboard is your command center for monitoring and managing student devices. This guide walks you through accessing it and understanding the layout.

**Time to complete:** ~5 minutes

---

## Step 1: Log in to Lightspeed Classroom

1. Open your browser and go to **[classroom.relay.school](https://classroom.relay.school)** (or use the Lightspeed link in your bookmarks bar).
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"** and use your **SCPS Google account**.
3. You'll land on the **My Classes** homepage.

!!! tip "Bookmark it"
    Add `classroom.relay.school` to your bookmarks bar so it's always one click away.

---

## Step 2: Understand the My Classes homepage

The My Classes page shows all of your classes as **tiles**. Each tile represents a class synced from Google Classroom.

**What you can do from here:**

- **Click a class tile** to open it and start monitoring
- **Drag and drop tiles** to reorder them in whatever way makes sense for your schedule
- **See student status** at a glance — each tile shows how many students are online

### Class tile status indicators

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Students shown with screen thumbnails | Class session is active, students are being monitored |
| Grayed out tile | No active session for this class |
| Number badge | Count of students currently online in that class |

---

## Step 3: Know the key areas of a class view

Once you click into a class, here's what you'll see:

### Top toolbar

The toolbar across the top gives you quick access to the main actions:

- **Web Rules** — Set allow/block lists for the class
- **Share Screen** — Broadcast your screen to students
- **Lock Screens** — Freeze all student devices
- **Push Link** — Send a URL to student browsers
- **Message** — Send a message to student devices

### Student tile area

The main area shows a **thumbnail of each student's screen** in real time. You can:

- **Zoom in/out** using the slider to see more or fewer screens at once
- **Click on a student** to zoom into their individual screen
- **Right-click a student** (or click the three dots) for individual actions

### Student status sections

Students are organized into sections:

| Section | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **My Class** | Students currently checked into your class and being monitored |
| **Available to Add** | Students who should be in your class but are checked into another teacher's session |
| **Offline** | Students whose devices are not currently online |

!!! info "Available to Add"
    If a student shows up in "Available to Add," it usually means another teacher's session is still running. You can drag and drop them into your class to take over monitoring.

---

## Step 4: Add a custom class (optional)

If you need to monitor students who aren't in one of your Google Classrooms (e.g., a study hall, advisory, or after-school group):

1. Hover over the space under **My Classes** on the homepage.
2. Click **"Add Class"**.
3. Name the class.
4. Add students by email address, or share a join code/link with them.
