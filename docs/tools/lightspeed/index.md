# Lightspeed Classroom

Lightspeed Classroom Management gives you real-time visibility into what students are doing on their devices during class. You can monitor screens, control web access, send messages, push links, and keep students focused — all from your own device.

---

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - Lightspeed Classroom syncs with your **Google Classroom** rosters. Make sure your Google Classrooms are set up and students have joined before using Lightspeed.
    - Student devices are **only monitored during active class sessions** on campus. Teachers cannot monitor students outside of school hours or off campus.
    - If you find a site that needs to be allowed or blocked at the **district filter level** (not just your class), submit a ticket through your building's IT process.
