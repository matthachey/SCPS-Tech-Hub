# Setting up your Mastery Tracker

The Mastery Tracker is the core of MasteryConnect — it's where you'll see student progress on each standard at a glance. This guide walks you through creating your first tracker.

**Time to complete:** ~10 minutes

---

## Before you begin

!!! warning "Prerequisites"
    - You must have an active MasteryConnect account (provided by SCPS)
    - You need to know which course and standards you'll be tracking
    - Your Canvas course should already be set up (see [Linking to Canvas](link-to-canvas.md) for the next step)

---

## Step 1: Log in to MasteryConnect

1. Go to [masteryconnect.com](https://masteryconnect.com) or access it through your **Canvas dashboard** via the MasteryConnect link.
2. Sign in with your SCPS credentials. If this is your first time, you may need to use the SSO (Single Sign-On) option tied to your SCPS Google account.

!!! tip "Bookmark it"
    Save the MasteryConnect login page to your bookmarks bar for quick access.

---

## Step 2: Create a new tracker

1. From your MasteryConnect dashboard, click **"Trackers"** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the **"+ New Tracker"** button (top right).
3. You'll be prompted to set up your tracker with the following information:

    | Field | What to Enter |
    |-------|---------------|
    | **Tracker Name** | Use a clear naming convention, e.g., `Smith - US History - Period 3` |
    | **Subject** | Select your subject area |
    | **Grade Level** | Select the appropriate grade |
    | **School Year** | Make sure the current school year is selected |

!!! tip "Naming convention"
    Using a consistent format like `LastName - Subject - Period` makes it much easier for you and your administrators to find trackers later.

---

## Step 3: Add standards to your tracker

This is the most important step — you're telling MasteryConnect which standards you want to track for this class.

1. After creating the tracker, click **"Add Standards"**.
2. Select **Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL)** as the standards set.
3. Browse or search for the specific standards for your course.
4. **Check the box** next to each standard you want to track.
5. Click **"Add Selected"** to attach them to your tracker.

!!! info "How many standards?"
    You don't need to add every SOL. Focus on the **power standards** or the standards your team has identified as priorities for the grading period. You can always add more later.

---

## Step 4: Organize your standards (optional but recommended)

Once your standards are added, you can drag and drop them to reorder them in a way that matches your pacing guide or curriculum map.

1. Click on a standard and **drag it** to reorder.
2. Standards at the top of the list will appear first in your tracker view.
3. Consider grouping them by **unit or reporting period** for easier navigation.

---

## Step 5: Add students

Students can be added to your tracker in two ways:

=== "Through Canvas (Recommended)"
    If you link your tracker to Canvas, students will sync automatically. This is the preferred method at SCPS. See [Linking MasteryConnect to Canvas](link-to-canvas.md).

=== "Manually"
    1. Open your tracker and click **"Students"**
    2. Click **"+ Add Students"**
    3. Search for students by name or ID number
    4. Select and add them to your tracker

!!! warning "Always link through Canvas"
    At SCPS, trackers **must be linked through Canvas** to ensure student access and accurate reporting. Manual entry should only be used as a temporary fix.
