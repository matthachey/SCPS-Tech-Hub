# Linking MasteryConnect to Canvas

Connecting your Mastery Tracker to your Canvas course is **required at SCPS**. This link ensures students can access assessments seamlessly and that reporting data stays consistent across both platforms.

**Time to complete:** ~5 minutes

---

## Before you begin

!!! warning "Prerequisites"
    - Your Mastery Tracker must already be created (see [Setting up your Mastery Tracker](setup-tracker.md))
    - Your Canvas course must be published and have students enrolled
    - You need to be logged in to both MasteryConnect and Canvas

---

## Step 1: Open your Mastery Tracker

1. Log in to MasteryConnect.
2. Navigate to **Trackers** in the left sidebar.
3. Click on the tracker you want to link.

---

## Step 2: Access the link settings

1. Inside your tracker, click the **gear icon** (:material-cog:) or **"Settings"** in the top right.
2. Select **"Link to Canvas"** or **"SIS Integration"** from the menu.

---

## Step 3: Select your Canvas course

1. A list of your available Canvas courses will appear.
2. Find and select the **matching course** (e.g., your Period 3 US History course).
3. Click **"Link"** to connect them.

!!! tip "Match carefully"
    Make sure you're linking the correct period/section. The tracker name and Canvas course name should match. If you used the naming convention from the [tracker setup guide](setup-tracker.md), this should be straightforward.

---

## Step 4: Verify the student sync

After linking, your Canvas roster should sync to MasteryConnect automatically.

1. Go back to your tracker and click **"Students"**.
2. Verify that your student list matches your Canvas roster.
3. If students are missing, check that they are **actively enrolled** in your Canvas course.

!!! info "Sync timing"
    The student sync may take a few minutes. If students don't appear right away, wait 5–10 minutes and refresh the page.

---

## Step 5: Test student access

Once linked, students can access MasteryConnect assessments directly through Canvas.

1. In your Canvas course, students will see MasteryConnect assessments as **assignments** (if you push them to Canvas) or can access them via a **testing code**.
2. Have a student verify they can see the assessment from their end.

---

## Troubleshooting

??? question "My Canvas course isn't showing up in the list"
    - Make sure your Canvas course is **published** (not in draft mode)
    - Verify you're logged into MasteryConnect with the same account linked to your Canvas
    - Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in

??? question "Students are missing from my tracker after linking"
    - Check that the students are actively enrolled in the Canvas course (not in a "pending" or "inactive" state)
    - Wait 5–10 minutes for the sync to complete
    - If the issue persists, contact your building ITRT

??? question "I linked to the wrong Canvas course"
    - Go to tracker **Settings** → **Link to Canvas**
    - **Unlink** the current course
    - Select the correct course and relink
