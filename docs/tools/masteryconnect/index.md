# MasteryConnect

MasteryConnect is the standards-based assessment and data platform used across SCPS to track student mastery of Virginia SOL standards. It integrates with Canvas to provide a seamless assessment experience.

---

## Getting Started

New to MasteryConnect? Start here.

| Guide | Time | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| [Setting up your Mastery Tracker](setup-tracker.md) | ~10 min | Create your first tracker and add standards |
| [Linking MasteryConnect to Canvas](link-to-canvas.md) | ~5 min | Connect your tracker to your Canvas course |

---

!!! info "SCPS-Specific Notes"
    - Trackers **must be linked through Canvas** for student access and reporting consistency.
    - MVPA (Mastery View Predictive Assessments) are pushed out from the district — you do not need to create these yourself.
    - Questions you author go into your **personal bank** and the **SCPS teacher bank** for others to use.
