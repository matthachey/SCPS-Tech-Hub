# Scoring with Bubble Sheets

Bubble sheets let you administer paper-based assessments while still capturing data in MasteryConnect. This is useful when devices are limited or when you want a low-tech testing experience.

**Time to complete:** ~5 minutes

---

## Before you begin

!!! warning "Prerequisites"
    - Your assessment must have **"Printable"** enabled (set during assessment creation)
    - You need a printer and scanner (or a device camera with the MasteryConnect scanning feature)

---

## Step 1: Download and print bubble sheets

1. Open your Mastery Tracker and go to the **Assessments** tab.
2. Find the assessment and click on it.
3. Look for the **"Bubble Sheets"** or **"Download Bubble Sheets"** option.
4. The bubble sheets will download as a PDF, **pre-slugged with your students' ID numbers**.
5. Print one bubble sheet per student.

!!! tip "Pre-slugged sheets save time"
    Because each bubble sheet already has the student's ID, you don't need students to fill in their information. Just distribute the correct sheet to each student.

---

## Step 2: Administer the assessment

1. **Print the assessment** itself (separate from the bubble sheets).
2. Distribute both the assessment and the matching bubble sheet to each student.
3. Students answer questions on the **bubble sheet**, not on the assessment itself.
4. Collect the bubble sheets when students are finished.

---

## Step 3: Scan the bubble sheets

MasteryConnect supports scanning through its web platform.

1. Go to your assessment in MasteryConnect.
2. Click **"Score"** or **"Scan Bubble Sheets"**.
3. Use your **document scanner** or **device camera** to scan each sheet.
4. MasteryConnect will read the bubbles and auto-score the responses.

!!! info "Scanning tips"
    - Make sure sheets are **flat and unwrinkled** for best scanning accuracy.
    - Scan in good lighting if using a camera.
    - If a scan doesn't register correctly, you can **manually enter** scores as a backup.

---

## Step 4: Verify and finalize scores

1. After scanning, review the scores in MasteryConnect.
2. Check for any **flagged responses** that the scanner couldn't read clearly.
3. Manually correct any errors.
4. Once everything looks good, the scores will automatically populate in your **Mastery Tracker**.
