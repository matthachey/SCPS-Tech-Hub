# About the SCPS Tech Hub

The SCPS Tech Hub is a collection of step-by-step instructional technology guides built specifically for Shenandoah County Public Schools teachers and staff.

## Who maintains this?

This site is maintained by the SCPS Instructional Technology Resource Teachers (ITRTs). Guides are written with SCPS-specific settings, workflows, and best practices in mind.

## Why does this exist?

Generic help documentation doesn't account for how *we* use our tools at SCPS. These guides are tailored to our division's configuration, our students, and our instructional priorities.
